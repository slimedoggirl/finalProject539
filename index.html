About Me

  • Archivist
  • Artist
  • Gamer
  • Student

My name is Mary Clare, but you can call me MC. I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Information at the University of Michigan, with a focus on libraries and archives. My areas of interest particularly revolve around information access and the process of creating memories in digital environments.



When not in class, I work as a student processing archivist at the Bentley Historical Library. Additionally, I serve as a research assistant to Dr. Sarita Schoenebeck, where my work involves studying and archiving social media platforms.



In past lives, I worked as a geochemist, sustainability activist, jewelry maker, and dog sitter. Check out related links to see my work and more.
